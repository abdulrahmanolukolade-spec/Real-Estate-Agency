About LUXENEST

Two decades of excellence in luxury real estate.

Our Story

Redefining Luxury
Real Estate Since 2001

Founded with a vision to transform the luxury real estate experience, LUXENEST has grown from a boutique agency into one of the most respected names in high-end property worldwide.

Our journey began in the heart of New York City, where we recognized that discerning clients deserved more than just a transaction — they deserved a partnership built on trust, discretion, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

Today, with offices in 12 cities across 6 continents, we continue to set the standard for luxury real estate services, representing the world's most exceptional properties and clientele.
